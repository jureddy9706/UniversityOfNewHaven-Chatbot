 Feb 28, 2025 Internet of Things Certificate Program Coordinator: Junhui Zhao, Ph.D. It is estimated that by the year 2030, the number of Internet-connected devices will exceed 50 billion. The graduate certificate program in IoT is designed for working professionals who wish to learn the concepts and the skills needed to design and implement IoT solutions. This certificate program consists of four courses (12 credits). Based on competency levels, the two fundamental courses (ELEC 6602 and ELEC 6610) may be replaced with others such as Cloud Computing, Introduction to Computer Security, or Wireless Networks. Consult the program advisor for approval of possible substitutions.
