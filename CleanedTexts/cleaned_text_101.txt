 Feb 28, 2025 Business Management Certificate Advisor: Robert Albright, Ph.D. This certificate develops students' conceptual knowledge and skills in formulating corporate strategy and in determining structural and resource requirements. The courses focus on concepts and processes useful in relation to general management and on functional responsibilities in coordinating and directing the organizational effort in our ever-changing economic environment. Some of the courses in the certificate have prerequisites; consult course descriptions elsewhere in this catalog.* Required Courses MGMT 6637 - Management ProcessPlus three of the following: MGMT 6645 - Management of Human ResourcesMGMT 6655 - Corporate Governance and Business StrategyMGMT 6662 - Organization TheoryMGMT 6664 - Organizational EffectivenessMGMT 6670 - Selected TopicsNote(s):Other management courses may be permitted as substitutions with approval of the advisor. *M.P.A. students should complete 12 credits of the core curriculum in the M.P.A. program, including PADM 6601 and PADM 6625 , as the prerequisite for this certificate.
