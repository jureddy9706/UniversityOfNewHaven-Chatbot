 Feb 28, 2025 Healthcare Informatics Certificate A certificate in healthcare informatics seeks to foster student success in data analysis, decision making, and problem solving by applying quantitative analytical tools and rational models in a healthcare administration setting. Students will demonstrate competence in understanding health systems and organizations, health information technology, revenue cycle management, and health analytics.
