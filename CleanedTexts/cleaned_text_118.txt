 Feb 28, 2025 Financial Crimes Investigation Certificate Advisor: Patrick Malloy, Senior Lecturer The graduate certificate program in financial crimes investigation is comprised of one main required course and three electives. The three electives can be focused areas of study or a broader open selection that explore multiple areas of financial crimes investigations. Students should discuss courses with their advisor. Online only.
