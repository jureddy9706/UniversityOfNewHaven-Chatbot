 Feb 28, 2025 Healthcare Improvement Certificate A certificate in healthcare improvement seeks to foster student success in initiating, planning, executing, monitoring, and controlling quality improvement projects in a healthcare setting. Students will demonstrate competence in understanding health systems and organizations, organizational climate and culture, project management, and principles of Lean Six Sigma.
