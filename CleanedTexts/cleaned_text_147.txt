 Feb 28, 2025 Sports Integrity Investigations Certificate Advisor: Patrick Malloy, Senior Lecturer U.S. and international sports face an unprecedented wave of corruption, money-laundering, organized crime, doping, gambling-related crime, and sexual/physical abuse. The program gives students the ability to recognize and combat these forms of corruption and helps prepare them for potential employment in the new industry of sports integrity monitoring. The sports integrity investigations certificate is comprised of four required courses.
