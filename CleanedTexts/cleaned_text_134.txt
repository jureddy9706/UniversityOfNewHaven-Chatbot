 Feb 28, 2025 Lean/Six Sigma Certificate This certificate provides a solid foundation in Lean Six Sigma concepts and tools that are essential to process improvement in manufacturing or service systems. Students will be equipped with knowledge and skills to apply Lean Six Sigma principles and methodologies to identify, define, analyze, and develop solutions for process improvement related problems. The certificate is a 12-credit program and is comprised of four courses.
