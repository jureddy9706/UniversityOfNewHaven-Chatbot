 Feb 28, 2025 Digital Forensic Investigations, M.S. The Master of Science in Digital Forensic Investigations combines the expertise of the investigations program with leading experts in digital forensic investigations and E-Discovery. The program integrates the legal and prosecution issues with the exploration of digital forensic tools commonly used in digital forensic investigations. 30 credit hours NOTE: This program is offered in an online format only. PROGRAM OUTCOMES: The student will be able to describe the steps necessary for a thorough investigation. The student will be able to identify correct investigative tools and information sources used in investigations. The student will be able to define the legal requirements for investigations. The student will be able to critique an investigation.
