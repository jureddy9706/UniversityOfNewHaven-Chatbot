 Feb 28, 2025 Forensic Mental Health Certificate Advisor: Melissa L. Whitson, Ph.D., Columbia University The 12-credit advanced training program is designed for students who have achieved a master's degree applicable to counseling or related field and need to supplement their coursework in the management and care of individuals in forensic settings. It is designed to enhance the knowledge and skills of professionals currently working or who will be working in forensic mental health settings. Program Outcomes Upon completion of this certificate, students will be able to: Utilize psychological concepts and principles to explain human behavior within the criminal justice system. Formulate forensic treatment plans.
