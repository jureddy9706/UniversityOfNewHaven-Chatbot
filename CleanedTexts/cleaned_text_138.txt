 Feb 28, 2025 National Security Certificate This 12-credit certificate is designed to provide students the ability to explore and combine aspects of the theoretical foundations of national security at both the domestic and international levels, and provide students with the core skills and knowledge necessary to craft national security policy. Program Outcomes: Evaluate national security threats at both domestic and international levels. Apply national security concepts to real-world scenarios. Required Courses NSEC 6601 - National Security Architecture, Mission, and TheoryNSEC 6603 - National Security Charter, Legal Issues, and Executive OrdersTwo restricted electives (6 credits total) chosen from NSEC, PADM, CJST, INVS, EMGT, CSCI or DSCI courses.
