For more information, please see https://www.newhaven.edu/admissions/stem-designated-programs.php. The MPS in User Experience (UX) Design integrates a set of research and technical tools, user-centered design, leadership and team building, critical thinking and practical skills that will empower students to be tomorrow's leaders in the user experience profession. These competencies provide students with the skills needed to become employed in a wide range of careers in UX design. This program requires the completion of 30 credits.
