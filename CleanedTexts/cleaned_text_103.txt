 Feb 28, 2025 Commercial Credit Risk Management Certificate This program will provide banking loan officer candidates with requisite skills and knowledge to make informed lending decisions while considering the regulatory environment in which the lending institution operates. The program includes asset, liability, and enterprise risk management considerations in financial services industries. Lending decisions in conjunction with commercial and real estate customers will also be addressed. Students with prior financial accounting coursework are able to waive the Accounting Foundations requirement. Consent of the department chairperson is required.
