 Feb 28, 2025 Global Health Certificate In this certificate program, students will explore the challenges of globalization and health equity around the world, and how ethics and human rights shape global health-focused research, programs, and policies. Students will design, implement, and evaluate global health programs to provide sustainable solutions for public health, prevent communicable disease, and undergo emergency preparedness efforts at local, national, and international levels.
