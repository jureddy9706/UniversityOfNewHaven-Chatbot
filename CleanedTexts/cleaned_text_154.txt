 Feb 28, 2025 Wildlife and Environmental Crimes Investigations Certificate Advisor: Patrick Malloy, Senior Lecturer From smuggling to poaching, wildlife crimes are increasing dramatically. The same can be said in the field of environmental crimes. International organized crime organizations, as well as terrorist groups are highly engaged in these activities. In conjunction with ManchesterCF, a leading international financial intelligence training provider, students in this certificate program will be provided the educational training to combat these crimes.
